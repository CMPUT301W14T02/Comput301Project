Help: Edit Comment

From this page you can edit the text, location, and picture attached to your comment.
By default the information of your previous comment are displayed.
To change the text of the comment enter your changes in the text box. To change the location of the comment enter the new coordinates in the latitude and longitude boxes. To take a new photo or replace your existing one click the camera button.
When you are happy with your changes, click the post button to submit your edited comment.
